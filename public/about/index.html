About ARAP

The Arkansas Accountability Project presents primary-source records and documented facts to support informed public discussion. ARAP does not treat the absence of a record as proof of wrongdoing and publishes corrections when factual errors are identified.

Editorial standard: If a statement cannot be supported by an official record, Arkansas statute, or other verifiable documentation, it is not included as a factual claim.
